This HIT is an academic experiment about making judgments about athletes' performances.

You will learn about the past performances of some athletes, and be asked to predict how well they will do in today's competition. Then, you will get to see how each athlete actually performed today, and you will be asked to determine how much responsibility the athlete should take for that performance. The whole procedure should take about than 20 minutes to complete.

After completing this HIT, you will receive a payment of $3.00. If you encounter any issues while attempting the HIT, or have feedback for us, please reach out to Kariyushi Rao (kariyushi@uchicago.edu). We try our best to follow up with everyone within 24 hours.
