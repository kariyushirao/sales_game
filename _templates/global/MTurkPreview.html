This HIT is an academic experiment. You will make judgments about athletes' performances in a tournament. It will take less than 15 minutes to complete (usually around 10 minutes).

You will learn about the past performances of some athletes, and you will see how each athlete performs in today's tournament. You will be asked to determine how much responsibility the athlete should take for his or her performance today. At the end, you'll answer 5 very brief questions about your judgments, and that will wrap up the HIT.

After completing this HIT, you will receive a payment of $2.00. If you encounter any issues while attempting the HIT, or have feedback for us, please reach out to Kariyushi Rao (kariyushi@uchicago.edu). We respond to every message within 24 hours.
