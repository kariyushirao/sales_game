In the real world, companies usually employ more than one sales person, and salespeople can share information about their past experience with customers to help each other decide what to pitch to customers in the future. In The Sales Game, you will be matched up with between 2 and 5 other players. At the end of each round, you and the other players in your group can choose whether or not to share your results from that round with each other.

If you and another player BOTH choose to share your results with each other, then you will BOTH be able to see each other's results while you are working on your next pitch. In other words, if Player X chooses to share their results with you, and you also choose to share with Player X, then you and Player X will see each other's results. However, if EITHER of you chooses NOT to share information with each other, then NEITHER of you will see each other's results. So, if Player X chooses to share their results with you, and you do not choose to share with Player X, then neither of you will see the other person's results.`

In each phase of the game, you and the other players in your group will all be working for the same company, but you will each face different customers. If you choose to share your results with each other, you can use the information about each other's results to learn about the average value of each platform.
