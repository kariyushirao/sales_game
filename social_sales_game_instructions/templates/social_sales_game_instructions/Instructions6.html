NOTE: During the real game, you will only have 60 seconds to submit your pitch. After 60 seconds, the page will automatically submit an empty pitch, and you will receive a Total Pitch Score of ZERO for that round. If you repeatedly fail to submit your responses before time runs out, you will be marked as a DROPOUT and you will not receive the $4.00 Active Participation Bonus. Make sure you always submit your pitch in less than 60 seconds.

Build your sales pitch for Company F

Starting with your second pitch for each company, you'll be able to see the results from the pitch you made in the previous round, and from the highest-scoring pitch you've made so far. You'll also have the choice to view each of the other players' results from the previous round if you BOTH chose to share your results with each other.

You can look at one player's results at a time. You can look at each player's results as many times as you want. If you want to reopen someone's results, just click on that player again. If you try to look at someone's results and you see the message "Not Shared," that means that either you did not choose to share with that player, that player chose not to share with you, or both of you chose not to share with each other.
