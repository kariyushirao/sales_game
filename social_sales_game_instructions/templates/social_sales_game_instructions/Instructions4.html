Build your sales pitch for Company F

Each round, you'll be asked to choose 3 platforms to include in your pitch. The video below demonstrates how this will work. To add a platform to your pitch, just click on the name of the platform in the Platform List on the left, then click the ADD button. The platform you selected will then move from the Platform List on the left to Your Pitch on the right.

If you change your mind about a platform after adding it to your pitch, just click on the name of the platform in Your Pitch on the right, then click the REMOVE button. The platform you selected will then move from Your Pitch back to the Platform List on the left. When you are done editing your pitch, click the blue SUBMIT button at the bottom of the page.
