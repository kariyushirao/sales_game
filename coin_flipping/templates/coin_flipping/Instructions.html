Coin Flipping Game

In this game you will watch our fair coin simulator flip a fair coin eight times. At the end of every eighth flip, you will be asked to predict what side the coin will land on next. After you make your prediction about the ninth flip, the simulator will restart and flip the coin another 8 times. This process will repeat 11 times, so you will watch the simulator produce 11 sequences of 8 flips, and make a prediction after each of these 11 sequences.
